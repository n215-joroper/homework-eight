Places:

Diagon Alley

Diagon Alley was a cobblestoned wizarding alley and shopping area located in London, England behind a pub called the Leaky Cauldron. Inside the alley was an assortment of restaurants, shops, and other sights. All items on the Hogwarts supply list could be bought at Diagon Alley.

The Greenhouse

The Hogwarts greenhouses were the location for the school's Herbology lessons and were full of wild and rare magical plants, including Mandrakes. Depending on what year the students were in, they would frequent different greenhouses corresponding to the level of Herbology they were learning.

The Forbidden Forest

The Forbidden Forest was an ominous wooded area, situated on the edges of Hogwarts but out-of-bound to its students. Full of magical creatures and beings, including unicorns, Acromantula, Thestrals and centaurs, the forest was known as a dangerous place for all - except perhaps Hagrid.
